About Me

Julia Claire — known to her clients as Juju — is a body piercer and jewelry curator based in Houston, TX, working out of Tattoo Temple Houston. She approaches piercing as a fine art, combining anatomical expertise with an eye for composition to create ear stacks and placements that are as intentional as they are beautiful.

Juju uses only implant-grade titanium and gold jewelry, maintaining the highest standards of safety and sterility so every client leaves feeling confident and cared for. Whether it's your very first piercing or a full ear transformation, she's here for every step of the journey — walk-ins welcome on Fridays and Saturdays.
